Miracle Rats is a "did you know it's just one guy!" sort of project from Lubin, Poland. She specialises in indie rock (not to be confused with indian rock). With her tracks she paints landscaped of autumn sunsets and wondering about your existance.


In March of 2025 she debuted with her album "Oh, Okay". The songs tell stories of procrastination, finding your identity and ancient god, who will destroy the world once he wakes up. You know, normal stuff.
As of writing this she's hard at work on her 2nd album "Goodbye, Shooting Star". The first single "Drive" is going to come out on October 1st!
