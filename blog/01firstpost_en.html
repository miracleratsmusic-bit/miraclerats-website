Where have I been?


Scince I released "Oh, Okay" in March I went on a hiatus. The main reason were my finals (that I have passed now :D). It was supposed to last until the end of the exams and then I was going to come back with a new single and all that jazz.

Well, that didn't happen.

I decided to focus on SKID, one of my bands. At this time we were cleaning the garage that is now the new HQ of The Lifeless Room Studios. We also sstarted recording our first album, which is still in production at the moment. At the end of May we played a gig with Fata Morgana and Destiny at CDT Lubin (my local culture centre) during the event "Młody Rock" (litrally "Young Rock"), which all happened, because I asked CDT if there wanted to host a gig. And by some unexpected conditions it was also SKID's first headlining gig. Pretty cool, right?
Full SKID's set on Youtube


Between SKID's recording sessions there were also Fata Morgana's band practice sessions. We got down some new songs, but we did't do much recording as we found it hard to organise meeting when everyone was going on holiday :(. Another project that has absorbed me over the summer was melinoë's (yes, all lowercase) debut album. We started working on it back in the Febuary of 2024, but got around to finishing it this summer. It comes out on September 26th, so mark the date in your calendars.


Pre-save "Sodom and Gomorah"

And after this brief product placement we're back in the present.

Right now I'm finishing SKID's album and I'm finilizing the songs for new Miracle Rats release. In the meantime, I'm recording an album with my friend Kajetan as Lazy Snails 22. Oh, and I'm getting ready to leave for college. I think that's important. Idk.

Aaaand that's all for now. For reading trough the whole post, I have a little surprise for you :3.



It's a cover for the first single from the upcoming album. Pretty cool, isn't it?

See you in the next one!
